Projects

This is my most recent project I've worked with a team of engineers. This was for the annual entrepreneurship competetion at our local high school. During the 2 previous years my team made it to the final round without winning an award. This year with so much determination and perserverence, we developed a product that would end our losing competition streak. This year we developed a series line of products that focused on improving access and ability to charge items. We developed the power pop, power table, and power pants. Each of these products emphasize wireless charging in a safe and efficient way. I worked on modeling the power pants in my team and we decided to use a wireless charger in an extra pcoket and run wiring through the inseams of the pants protected by a layer of chlorinated polyethylene to protect the wiring from damage in the washing machine. We also added a flexible solar panel such that users would be able to charge their phones while walking outside. We were able to fully complete the model with one issue being that we purchased the wrong amperage solar panel so we had issues with the sun generating enough power. However, after using a power source in replacement of the solar panel we were able to get it to produce power for a charger. We also developed the circuitry and models of what would be a table that could safely charge multiple items safely and a portable solar charger. This year was one of my most successful years with my team ending as a finalist. We also found it funny that apple came out with the similar product as the power table a little bit after the competition with there model being a mat. I'm so proud of the work I've done this project as I embraced learning the circuit design of a wireless charger while also using my skills in chemistry and business to develop a cost efficient solution within a team. If my team and I weren't going to different universities we would continue pursuing this model and we would continue testing till we made a sustainable option for society.
